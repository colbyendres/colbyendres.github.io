Selected Projects

Below is a portfolio of my projects, collaborative work, and experiments. Each card captures the scope, timeline, and an at-a-glance summary. For more information, check out the Musings page which offers a deeper dive in to many of these projects.

  • QEDSports

    Completed: Jan 2026

    Used graph analytics and artificial intelligence to generate explanations for predicting winners of college football matchups. Loosely inspired by My Team Is Better Than Your Team.

  • Astra

    Completed: Aug 2025

    Leveraged the SPECTER embedding model to generate follow-up recommendations for a given research paper. Recommendations were drawn from a 60k+ paper dataset curated from arXiv.

  • Cacheus

    Completed: May 2025

    Implemented the Cacheus cache replacement policy for the ZSim simulator. Experimented with various learning rate schedulers, yielding an approximate 5% improvement in miss rate over classical policies.

  • AggieAssign

    Completed: Dec 2024

    Developed a full-stack application for generating class schedules for the computer science department at Texas A&M. Used linear programming techniques to design schedules maximizing instructor happiness and minimizing room waste. Reduced schedule creation time from multiple days to under a minute.
